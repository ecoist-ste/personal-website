Competitions

COMAP'S High School Mathematical Contest in Modeling (HiMCM) is an international contest designed to provide high school students with the opportunity to work as team members to engage in and improve their modeling, problem solving, and writing skills.

Shing-Tung Yau, the S. -T. Yau High School Science Award inspires scientific innovations and is designed for high school students all over the world. Advocating innovative thinking and collaborative spirits, the Award gives students the opportunity to participate by submitting academic papers.

6th Highest Overall Score, USAD China In-Country Competition

The Academic Decathlon is an annual high school academic competition organized by the non-profit United States Academic Decathlon. The competition consists of seven objective multiple choice tests, two subjective performance events, and an essay.

Excellent Award, Youth Impact Communication Initiative

YIC asks participants to be sensitive to societal problems remaining in their households, communities, cities, or even countries. After determining one particular topic as the central goal of their project, participants need to use statistics or other academic tools to give their own insights and finally form a paper.
